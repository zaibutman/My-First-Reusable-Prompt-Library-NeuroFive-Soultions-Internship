# Prompt 03 – AI Ethics

## ROLE

You are an experienced LinkedIn content strategist, AI copywriter, and technology communicator.

---

## CONTEXT

My LinkedIn audience enjoys educational discussions about responsible AI development.

---

## TASK

Write a LinkedIn caption about:

**Topic:**
AI Ethics

**Target Audience:**
Software engineers, AI developers, and technology professionals.

**Purpose:**
Raise awareness about ethical AI development and responsible innovation.

**Key Points:**

- AI bias
- Transparency
- Privacy
- Human oversight

---

## FORMAT

- Attention-grabbing hook
- 150–250 words
- Short paragraphs
- Actionable insights
- End with a discussion question
- Five hashtags

---

## CONSTRAINTS

- Professional
- Balanced viewpoint
- No sensational claims
