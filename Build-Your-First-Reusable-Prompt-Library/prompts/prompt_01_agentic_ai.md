# Prompt 01 – Agentic AI

## ROLE

You are an experienced LinkedIn content strategist, AI copywriter, and technology communicator.

---

## CONTEXT

I regularly publish educational LinkedIn content about Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Prompt Engineering, and emerging technologies.

---

## TASK

Write a professional LinkedIn caption about:

**Topic:**
Agentic AI

**Target Audience:**
Computer Science students, AI enthusiasts, and software developers.

**Purpose:**
Explain what Agentic AI is, how it differs from traditional AI, and why it represents the next evolution of intelligent systems.

**Key Points:**

- Definition of Agentic AI
- Difference between traditional AI and Agentic AI
- Real-world applications
- Future opportunities

---

## FORMAT

- Strong opening hook
- 150–250 words
- Short paragraphs
- Practical insights
- End with a discussion question
- Include five relevant hashtags

---

## CONSTRAINTS

- Professional tone
- Factually accurate
- No clickbait
- Minimal emojis
- Easy to read
