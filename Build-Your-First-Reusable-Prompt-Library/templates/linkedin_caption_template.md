# LinkedIn Caption Generator Template

## Template Information

**Template Name:** Professional LinkedIn Caption Generator

**Version:** 1.0

**Category:** Social Media Content

**Purpose:** Generate professional, engaging, and informative LinkedIn captions for technology-related topics.

---

# Prompt Template

## ROLE

You are an experienced LinkedIn content strategist, AI copywriter, and technology communicator with expertise in creating engaging professional content.

---

## CONTEXT

I regularly publish LinkedIn posts about Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Prompt Engineering, Data Science, Software Development, and Career Development.

My audience includes:

- University Students
- AI Enthusiasts
- Software Engineers
- Developers
- Recruiters
- Technology Professionals

The goal is to educate, inspire, and encourage meaningful discussions while maintaining a professional tone.

---

## TASK

Write a LinkedIn caption about:

**Topic:**
`{{TOPIC}}`

Target Audience:

`{{AUDIENCE}}`

Purpose:

`{{PURPOSE}}`

Key Points to Include:

`{{KEY_POINTS}}`

---

## FORMAT

Generate a LinkedIn caption that includes:

- An engaging opening hook.
- A clear explanation of the topic.
- Practical insights or key takeaways.
- Short paragraphs for readability.
- A concluding question to encourage engagement.
- Five relevant hashtags.

---

## CONSTRAINTS

- Length: 150–250 words.
- Maintain a professional and conversational tone.
- Keep the content factually accurate.
- Avoid clickbait.
- Avoid excessive emojis.
- Do not use overly technical language unless necessary.
- Make the post easy to read on LinkedIn.
- Do not invent statistics or unsupported claims.

---

# Variables

| Variable | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| `{{TOPIC}}` | Main subject of the LinkedIn post |
| `{{AUDIENCE}}` | Intended readers |
| `{{PURPOSE}}` | Goal of the post |
| `{{KEY_POINTS}}` | Important ideas to include |

---

# Example Variable Values

## TOPIC

Prompt Engineering

## AUDIENCE

Computer Science Students

## PURPOSE

Teach beginners why prompt engineering is an essential AI skill.

## KEY_POINTS

- Definition of prompt engineering
- Importance in Generative AI
- Real-world applications
- Career opportunities

---

# Expected Output

The AI should generate:

- A professional LinkedIn caption.
- Well-structured paragraphs.
- Educational and engaging content.
- A discussion question.
- Five relevant hashtags.

---

# Notes

This template is reusable. To create a new prompt, simply replace the values of:

- `{{TOPIC}}`
- `{{AUDIENCE}}`
- `{{PURPOSE}}`
- `{{KEY_POINTS}}`

The overall prompt structure remains unchanged, ensuring consistency while supporting multiple LinkedIn post topics.
